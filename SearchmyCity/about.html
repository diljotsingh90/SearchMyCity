    About Us

    SearchmyCity project is developed for providing ease for user in searching variousplaces in their locality even the locations which are not present on GoogleMaps(less famous places).It is very helpful for someone  new in a city. He/she could search the desiredplace in the city or find from a list of available places for their need andgain abundant knowledge about them such as Temples, Hospitals, Schools,Colleges, Business like property advisors, shops, advocates, and grocery storesetc. in their respective cities or towns.

    These lists or posts canbe easily updated by any resident of the city who has good knowledge as well asthe developers of the project the owners of the specific locations can post aswell. For Example, A shopkeeper if does not see his/her shop’s information onthe website can post it. It can also be helpful for advertisement purposes.
